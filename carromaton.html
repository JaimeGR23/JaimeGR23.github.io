  • Watch Gameplay

Overview

Carromaton is a Unity drift racing prototype built under strict constraints: the game is fully controlled only with two joysticks. Developed by two programmers over three months, the project delivers responsive drift mechanics, engine audio + skid FX, Cinemachine transitions, and two distinct environments: a forest drift circuit and a city at night lit by glowing mushrooms.

Key Features

Both developers contributed equally to all aspects of the project. The highlights below showcase the most relevant features.

Dual Joystick Exclusive Controls

The entire game is designed to be played only with two joysticks. One press switches between third-person and top-down views smoothly using Cinemachine; the other joystick press returns the player to the lobby hub.

Forest Drift Circuit

A compact daytime drift track surrounded by stylised forest visuals. Focus on lap records, rewarding precise control and polished driving feel with skid marks and engine sound FX.

Night City with Luminous Mushrooms

The second world is larger and set at night. Cars switch on headlights, and the environment is filled with bioluminescent mushrooms, creating a distinct aesthetic atmosphere.

Tech keywords: Unity C#, Cinemachine, physics-based drift handling, skid FX, audio design, environment lighting, joystick input constraints.
