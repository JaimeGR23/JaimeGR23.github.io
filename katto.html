Katto: Rising Tides

Unreal Engine 5 | 3D Platformer built by a multidisciplinary team, focusing on polished mechanics and visual quality

Steam Release & GDWC Finalist

  • View on Steam

Overview

Katto: Rising Tides is a 3D platforming game developed in Unreal Engine 5 and released on Steam. The project was selected as a finalist for the GDWC 2024 in the Best Hobby Game category. The game combines precise platforming challenges with a unique water-themed aesthetic and dynamic camera work, aiming for a polished and professional player experience.

  • Platform: PC (with controller support)
  • Release date: July 4th, 2024
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Arcadia Games team: 7 Artists, 2 Designers, 7 Programmers, 5 Musicians
  • Development time: ~7 months
  • Finalist – GDWC 2024 Best Hobby Game

Contributions

As Graphics & Gameplay Programmer, I worked on a wide range of features that defined the player experience. The examples below highlight some of the most impactful systems I implemented — though they do not cover all of my contributions.

Dynamic Platforms

Implemented moving, rotating, collapsing, and slippery platforms that form the core of the platforming gameplay.

Advanced Camera System

Developed cinematic sequences, dynamic follow cameras, and trigger-based guiding cameras to improve gameplay flow and player orientation.

Particle VFX

Designed and integrated custom particle effects for water splashes and environmental feedback, adding polish and clarity to gameplay.

Custom Shader

Created a shader solution that ensures the player remains visible through obstructing geometry, improving playability in third-person view.

Additional Contributions

  • Developed interaction systems: buttons, doors, triggers.
  • Integrated art assets from the art team into the engine.
  • Collaborated with the design team on gameplay decisions and iteration.
  • Contributed to performance optimisation across the project.
